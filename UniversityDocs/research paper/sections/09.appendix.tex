\appendix
\section{Some further guidelines that go without saying (right?)}
\label{sec:appendix}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Follow the required page limit and formatting constraints (maximum 8 pages, two-column format).
  \item Ensure that the paper clearly communicates the central research goal and contributions.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Reference use}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Use a numbered citation style (IEEE format), where references appear as \cite{example}, [2]\ldots
  \item Use BibTeX or a similar tool to automatically generate bibliographic information.
  \item Every citation in the text must appear in the bibliography, and no uncited references should be included.
  \item All ideas, figures, and data derived from other work must be properly referenced.
  \item Literal quotations must include quotation marks and page numbers where applicable.
  \item Paraphrased content must be sufficiently distinct from the original source.
  \item Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Structure}
Paragraphs
\begin{itemize}
  \item are well-constructed and logically organized
  \item are not too long: each paragraph discusses a single idea
  \item start with clear topic sentences
  \item contribute to a coherent flow from research question to conclusions
  \item support claims with appropriate references and evidence
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Style}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Use a formal academic tone throughout the paper
  \item Prefer ``we'' over ``I'' when referring to the authors
  \item Avoid informal language and subjective phrasing
  \item Avoid unsupported or overly strong claims
  \item Be precise and cautious in wording (e.g., do not claim ``privacy achieved'' when only proxies are used)
  \item Ensure correct use of English: clear grammar, no spelling or lexical errors
  \item Maintain clarity: avoid overly complex or ambiguous sentences
  \item Use varied sentence structure with a mix of active and passive voice
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Tables and figures}
\begin{itemize}
  \item All tables and figures must be numbered (e.g., Figure 1, Table 1)
  \item Each must include a clear and descriptive caption
  \item Every table and figure must be referenced at least once in the text
  \item Figures and tables must be interpretable independently of the main text
  \item Avoid overly dense or cluttered visualizations
  \item If adapted or copied, include proper references
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Page limit and formatting}
\begin{itemize}
  \item The paper must not exceed 8 pages in two-column format
  \item Use consistent font, spacing, and layout throughout the document
  \item Follow a standard conference-style formatting
  \item Prioritize clarity and conciseness over excessive detail
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Key focus of the paper}
\begin{itemize}
  \item The paper must clearly demonstrate how embedded deployment constraints (such as model size and latency) reshape memory--accuracy--latency trade-offs in AI systems
  \item All sections should support and contribute to this central theme
\end{itemize}
